Our story

Who we are

KHM Law Firm was founded on a shared vision — to build a dynamic, well-managed legal practice centred on clear communication with clients, genuine commitment to every case, and professional excellence.

We are legal advisors (radcowie prawni) with many years of experience. Our academic connections and practical expertise combine to guarantee the quality of our services, which focus primarily on providing legal support to businesses and entrepreneurs, as well as individuals.

Our office is located in the heart of Kraków, and we operate throughout Poland.
