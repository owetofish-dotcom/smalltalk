Legal Advisor registered with the Regional Chamber of Legal Advisors in Kraków.

Graduate of the Faculty of Law, Jagiellonian University. During her studies she was an active member of the Civil Section of the Student Legal Clinic at the Jagiellonian University. She has over nine years of professional experience working at law firms in Kraków.

Areas of practice

Handles ongoing legal support for businesses, in particular contract analysis, drafting and negotiation.

Specialises in banking law with a focus on consumer protection. Represents clients in court proceedings against financial market entities.
