Corporate Legal Services

We support companies at every stage of their life: formation, ongoing governance, capital changes, restructuring and liquidation. A single legal partner for all your corporate needs.

Corporate Legal Services for Companies

Corporate law governs the internal life of a company: how it is formed, how decisions are made, how ownership changes, and how the company is transformed or wound up. Our corporate legal services cover all these areas for limited liability companies (sp. z o.o.), joint-stock companies (S.A.), simple joint-stock companies (P.S.A.), and partnerships of all types.

Company formation

We assist with choosing the right legal form and handle the entire incorporation process:

  • Drafting articles of association or statutes, including bilingual (PL/EN) versions
  • Advising on share structure, governance bodies and voting arrangements
  • Filing for registration in the National Court Register (KRS)
  • Obtaining NIP and REGON numbers, filing CRBR notifications
  • Preparing ancillary documents: powers of attorney, opening bank account authorisations, lease agreements for the registered office

Ongoing corporate governance

We provide continuous support for the day-to-day corporate life of a company:

  • Preparing and organising shareholders' meetings and general meetings
  • Drafting resolutions of shareholders, the management board and the supervisory board
  • Preparing minutes and protocols from meetings
  • Advising management board members on their duties and liability
  • Reviewing and updating internal corporate documents (rules of procedure, proxy grants)
  • Filing KRS updates (changes of address, board composition, share capital, etc.)

Share transactions and ownership changes

  • Share sale and purchase agreements (with or without representations and warranties)
  • Shareholder agreements (SHA): pre-emption rights, tag-along, drag-along, lock-up provisions
  • Capital increases and decreases
  • Admission of new shareholders and restructuring of ownership
  • Employee share schemes and management incentive plans

Mergers, acquisitions and restructuring

  • Mergers by absorption and by formation of a new company
  • Demergers and spin-offs (full and partial)
  • Transformation of a company from one legal form into another
  • Cross-border mergers and conversions
  • Enterprise acquisitions (purchase of a going concern)

Liquidation and dissolution

  • Voluntary liquidation proceedings from resolution to striking off the register
  • Appointment of liquidators and preparation of liquidation documentation
  • Handling creditor claims and distributing remaining assets
  • Filing for deletion from KRS

We work with companies of all sizes, from single-shareholder start-ups to complex multi-entity groups. We operate remotely across Poland, with the option of meetings at our office in central Kraków.

If you need legal support for your company's corporate matters, contact us. We will propose the right approach and get started quickly.
