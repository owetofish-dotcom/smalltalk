What we do

Our practice areas

We cover the full spectrum of legal needs for businesses, from day-to-day operations to complex corporate transactions.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Company formation, shareholders' agreements, corporate governance, mergers and transformations, business succession.

Banking Law

Advice on financing structures, bank lending, security documentation, and regulatory compliance for financial institutions and their clients.

Debt Recovery

Pre-litigation and litigation debt recovery, writ-of-payment proceedings, enforcement, and creditor representation before courts.

Foundations & Associations

Establishing and servicing foundations, associations, and public benefit organisations: legal, organisational and statutory matters.

Consumer Bankruptcy

Comprehensive support throughout consumer bankruptcy proceedings, from petition to debt discharge and fresh financial start.

Real Estate & Construction Law

Legal status of real estate, construction process advisory, property tax, investment transactions and development agreements.
